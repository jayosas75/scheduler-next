# Login Instructions

## Accessing the Scheduler

1. **Navigate to Login**
   - Open your browser and go to the scheduler application
   - You will be automatically redirected to `/login` if not authenticated

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - **Email**: Enter your registered email address
   - **Password**: Enter your password (minimum 6 characters)

3. **Submit**
   - Click the **"⊕ LOG IN"** button
   - If credentials are correct, you'll be redirected to your calendar

4. **First Time User?**
   - Click **"Create Account"** link to register
   - You'll be taken to the registration page

## Troubleshooting

- **Invalid Credentials**: Double-check your email and password
- **Forgot Password**: Currently no password reset (feature coming soon)
- **Account Locked**: Contact administrator

## Security Notes

- Your password is hashed and stored securely
- Sessions expire after inactivity
- Use **"⊗ SIGN OUT"** button when finished
